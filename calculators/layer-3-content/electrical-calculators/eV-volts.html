eV to Volts Calculator

Convert energy in electronvolts (eV) to electric potential in volts (V). This calculator assumes the charge of a single electron: 1 eV = 1.602176634 × 10⁻¹⁹ joules.

Formula:

  • V = E / q
  • Where:
    • V = Voltage (volts)
    • E = Energy (joules)
    • q = Charge (coulombs) = 1.602176634 × 10⁻¹⁹ C
