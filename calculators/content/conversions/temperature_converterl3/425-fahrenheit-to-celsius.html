Quick Answer: What is 425°F in Celsius?

425 degrees Fahrenheit is equal to approximately 218.33 degrees Celsius.

Formula to Convert 425°F to Celsius

To convert from Fahrenheit to Celsius, use the formula:

°C = (°F − 32) × 5/9

Applying the formula:


°C = (425 − 32) × 5/9
°C = 393 × 5/9
°C = 218.33°C (rounded to two decimal places)

Historical Perspective: Fahrenheit vs. Celsius

The Fahrenheit scale was created by physicist Daniel Gabriel Fahrenheit in 1724 and became standard in English-speaking countries for centuries. However, the Celsius scale, developed by Anders Celsius in 1742, gained popularity internationally due to its simplicity and alignment with the metric system. While 425°F is a common temperature in American ovens, most global equivalents are expressed in Celsius—hence the need for accurate conversion tools.
