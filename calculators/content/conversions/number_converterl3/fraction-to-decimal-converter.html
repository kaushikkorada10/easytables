What is a Fraction?

A fraction is a way of expressing a part of a whole. It has two parts:

  • Numerator: The top number, representing how many parts you have
  • Denominator: The bottom number, representing how many parts the whole is divided into

Example: 3/4 means you have 3 parts out of 4 equal parts.

What is a Decimal?

A decimal is another way of expressing fractions using base-10 notation. It‚Äôs easier to compare, calculate, and display decimals in scientific, technical, and financial contexts.

Example: 3/4 = 0.75 in decimal form.

Fraction to Decimal Formula

To convert a fraction to a decimal, divide the numerator by the denominator:

Examples:

  • 1/2 = 1 √∑ 2 = 0.5
