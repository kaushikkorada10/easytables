🔧 Pro Tips for Precision Use

  • Use **parentheses** to control operation order, e.g., (1h + 30m) × 2 vs. 1h + (30m × 2).
  • Suffix decimal notation—like 1.25h for 1 hour 15 minutes—to avoid manual conversion.
  • Combine days: 1d 2.5h will correctly handle 0.5 hours as 30 minutes.
  • Use negative numbers for countdowns: –1h 30m — ideal for “time left” displays.
  • Mix units freely: 2h 15m + 1500s all normalized in output.
