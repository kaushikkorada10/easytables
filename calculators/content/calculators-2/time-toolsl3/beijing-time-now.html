What Is Beijing Time?

Beijing operates under China Standard Time (CST), which is set at UTC+8:00 year-round. Despite spanning five geographical time zones, China maintains a single official time—a policy in place since 1949 to promote national unity.

  • Abbreviation: CST (China Standard Time)
  • Offset: UTC+8:00, no Daylight Saving Time

Understanding Time in Greater China

Although China has one official time zone, regions like Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan have unique identities:

Despite differing nomenclatures, clocks remain unified with Beijing — simplifying business, communication, and transport across these territories.
