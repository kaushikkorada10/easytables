What is a Date Calculator?

A Date Calculator is a utility that lets you compute the number of days, weeks, months, or years between two dates. You can also add or subtract a custom number of days to/from a starting date. It's perfect for handling calendars, deadlines, anniversaries, retirement estimates, pregnancy planning, and countless other real-world scenarios.

Core Features

  • Date Difference: Calculate the exact number of days, weeks, or months between two given dates.
  • Add/Subtract Days: Quickly find what date it will be after adding or subtracting a specific number of days.
  • Future/Past Date Finder: Enter a base date and a number to jump forward or backward in time.
  • Working Days (Coming Soon): Calculate business days excluding weekends and holidays.
  • Recurring Date Calculations: Determine recurring events such as “every 90 days from today.”

Date Calculator vs Manual Calculation

Manual date math is prone to errors due to leap years, varying month lengths, and holidays. Our calculator handles all of that for you. It accounts for:

  • Leap years (e.g., February 29)
  • Different month lengths (28, 30, 31 days)
  • Cross-year and multi-year differences

How It Works (Behind the Scenes)

We use JavaScript’s native Date() objects and libraries like dayjs or date-fns to ensure precision and timezone-safe computations. Your input is never stored — everything runs securely and instantly in your browser.

Fun Facts About Time & Dates

  • The Unix Epoch time started on January 1, 1970 at 00:00:00 UTC.
