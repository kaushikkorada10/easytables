Why Thailand Doesn’t Use Daylight Saving Time

Official Date & Time Notation in Thailand

Thailand uses the 24‑hour clock in official contexts (e.g., transportation, computing), while daily conversation often uses a unique **six‑hour system**:

  • ตี 1–6: 01:00–06:00 (early morning)
  • โมงเช้า 1–6: 07:00–12:59 (morning hours)
  • บ่าย 1–3 / โมงเย็น: 13:00–18:59 (afternoon/evening)
  • ทุ่ม 1–6: 19:00–00:59 (night time)
