Fraction Simplifier

This calculator reduces any proper or improper fraction to its simplest form by dividing the numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor (GCD).

  • Numerator = Top part of the fraction
  • Denominator = Bottom part of the fraction
  • GCD = Greatest Common Divisor of numerator and denominator

üìò What is Fraction Simplification?

Fraction simplification (or reduction) means rewriting a fraction so that the numerator and denominator have no common factor except 1. For example:

  • Original Fraction: 18/24
  • Simplified Form: 3/4

This is done by dividing both the numerator and the denominator by their greatest common divisor (GCD).
