‚ûó What is Division?

Division is one of the four fundamental operations of arithmetic, alongside addition, subtraction, and multiplication. It is the process of distributing a number (called the dividend) into a specified number of equal parts (defined by the divisor).

The result of a division operation is called the quotient, and any remaining value is known as the remainder.

Formula: Dividend √∑ Divisor = Quotient (with or without Remainder)
