Sine Calculator

Calculate the sine of an angle using degrees or radians. This calculator supports both units and shows the result with 6 decimal precision.

  • Formula: sin(θ), where θ is the angle in radians
  • To convert degrees to radians: radians = degrees × (π ÷ 180)
