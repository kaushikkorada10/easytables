Standard Deviation Calculator

Use this calculator to compute the standard deviation of a list of numbers. Just enter numbers separated by commas.

  • Formula: σ = √(Σ(xᵢ - μ)² / N)
  • Where μ is the mean and N is the total number of values.
  • This tool uses population standard deviation.
