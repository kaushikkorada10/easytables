Some offers apply multiple discounts sequentially. For example, 10% off a product, followed by an additional 5% off using a promo code. The correct approach is:

  • Step 1: Apply first discount
  • Step 2: Apply second discount on the new subtotal

This is different from combining the percentages (which would result in inaccurate savings).
