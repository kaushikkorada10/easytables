AWG to mm Calculator

Convert American Wire Gauge (AWG) to millimeter (mm) diameter using the standard formula:

  • Input: AWG (0 to 40)
  • Output: Wire diameter in millimeters
  • Formula: mm = 0.005 × 92(36 - AWG)/39 × 25.4
