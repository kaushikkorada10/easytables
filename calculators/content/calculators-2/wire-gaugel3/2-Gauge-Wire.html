#2 Gauge Wire Calculator

This tool helps you understand the properties and performance of #2 AWG copper wire based on length and current.

  • Cross-sectional Area: 33.6 mm²
  • Diameter: 6.544 mm
  • Resistance per meter: 0.000156 Ω/m (copper)
  • Formula: Voltage Drop = 2 × Length × Resistance × Current
