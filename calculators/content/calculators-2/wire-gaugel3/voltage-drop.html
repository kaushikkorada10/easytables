Voltage Drop Calculator

This calculator estimates the voltage drop in a wire due to resistance when current flows through it.

  • Formula: V = 2 × I × L × R
  • V = Voltage drop in volts
  • I = Current in amperes
  • L = Length of wire in meters (one way)
  • R = Resistance per meter in ohms (Ω/m)
