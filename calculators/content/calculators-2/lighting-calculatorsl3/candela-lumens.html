Candela to Lumens Calculator

Convert luminous intensity in candela (cd) to luminous flux in lumens (lm), using beam angle.

Formula:

  • Lumens = 2 × π × Candela × (1 − cos(θ / 2))
  • Where θ = beam angle in degrees
