High School GPA Calculator

The High School GPA Calculator helps students easily compute their grade point average based on letter grades and credit hours. GPA is calculated on a 4.0 scale in most high schools.

How it works:

  • Assign numerical values to letter grades (A = 4.0, B = 3.0, etc.)
  • Multiply each class grade by the credit hours
  • Sum up the total grade points and divide by total credit hours

Use this calculator to determine your current GPA and what grades you need to reach your goals.
