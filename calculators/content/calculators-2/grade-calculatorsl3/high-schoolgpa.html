Unweighted vs Weighted GPA

Unweighted GPA: All classes are scored equally. The highest GPA is 4.0 regardless of class difficulty.

Weighted GPA: More challenging courses like Honors or AP are scored on a higher scale (up to 5.0). This system rewards students for taking rigorous coursework.
