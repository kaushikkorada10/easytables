The kWh to Watts Calculator helps you convert energy usage in kilowatt-hours (kWh) into power in watts (W). This is useful when you want to understand how much power was used over a specific time period or when estimating the continuous power load based on energy consumption.

Understanding the Basics: kWh and W

kWh (kilowatt-hour) is a unit of energy. It represents the amount of power used over time. W (watt) is a unit of power ‚Äî the rate at which energy is consumed or produced.

For example, if you run a 1000W (1 kW) device for 1 hour, you will consume 1 kWh of energy.

Conversion Formula: kWh to W

The basic formula is:

Watts (W) = (kWh √ó 1000) / Time (in hours)

Multiply kWh by 1000 to convert kilowatts to watts, then divide by the number of hours the energy was used.

Example Calculation

Suppose you consumed 5 kWh of energy over 10 hours:

W = (5 √ó 1000) / 10 = 500 watts
