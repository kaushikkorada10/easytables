Why Is Power Factor Important?

Power factor (PF) is critical in AC systems. It measures how effectively electrical power is being used. A lower power factor results in more current draw for the same power, which can lead to overheating and energy waste. Most commercial and industrial equipment operates at PF values between 0.7 to 0.95. Including this value in your calculation ensures real-world accuracy.
