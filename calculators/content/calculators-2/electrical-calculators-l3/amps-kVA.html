Amps to kVA Calculator

Use this tool to convert current in amps (A) to apparent power in kilovolt-amps (kVA) for both single-phase and three-phase systems.

Formula:

  • Single phase: kVA = (V × I) / 1000
  • Three phase: kVA = (√3 × V × I) / 1000
