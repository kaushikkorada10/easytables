Understanding Amps and Kilowatts

  • Amps (A): A measure of the flow of electric current. The higher the amperage, the more electricity is being drawn by a device.
  • Kilowatts (kW): A unit of real power, representing 1,000 watts. It indicates the amount of power consumed by electrical equipment to perform work.

Amps to kW Conversion Formula

The conversion from amps to kW depends on several factors including voltage, system type (single-phase or three-phase), and power factor.
