FAQ ‚Äì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is 1 kW equal to 1 kWh?
No. 1 kW is a unit of power, and 1 kWh is a unit of energy. If 1 kW of power is used for 1 hour, then it equals 1 kWh of energy.

Q: How do I reduce my kWh usage?
Use energy-efficient appliances, limit usage time, and turn off devices when not in use.

Q: Does this apply to solar panels too?
Yes! If a solar panel produces 2 kW and operates for 5 hours in sunlight, it generates 10 kWh of energy.
