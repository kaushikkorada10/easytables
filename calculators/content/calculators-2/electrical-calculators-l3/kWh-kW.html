What is the Difference Between kWh and kW?

kWh (kilowatt-hour) is a unit of energy ‚Äî it represents how much energy is consumed over a certain period. kW (kilowatt), on the other hand, is a unit of power ‚Äî it represents the rate at which energy is used at any given moment.

Example: If a 1 kW appliance runs for 3 hours, it will consume 3 kWh of energy.

Conversion Formula: kWh to kW

The formula to convert kWh to kW is:

kW = kWh / Time (in hours)

This formula helps determine the average power used over a time duration.

Example: 10 kWh used over 5 hours ‚Üí kW = 10 / 5 = 2 kW
