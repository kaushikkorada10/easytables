Understanding kWh vs kW: Key Concepts

kWh (Kilowatt-Hour) and kW (Kilowatt) are fundamental units in electrical engineering:

  • kW measures power, the rate at which energy is used (1 kW = 1000 W).
  • kWh measures energy, the total amount consumed over time (1 kWh = 1 kW delivered for 1 hour).

The Formula: Converting kWh to kW

The relationship between energy and power is:

Power (kW) = Energy (kWh) √∑ Time (h)

To convert a value in kWh into an equivalent power in kW, you divide by the number of hours over which the energy was consumed.
