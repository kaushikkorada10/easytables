What Is an Online Calendar?

An online calendar is a digital tool accessible through your web browser that allows you to view, schedule, and manage dates, events, and appointments. Unlike traditional wall or paper calendars, it offers interactive features such as reminders, color coding, repeating events, and more. It’s designed for convenience, collaboration, and real-time accessibility — all without the need to install anything.
