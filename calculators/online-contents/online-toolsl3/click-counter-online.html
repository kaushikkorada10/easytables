Choosing the Right Chart Type for Your Data

One of the most common questions is: “Which chart should I use?” Here’s a quick guide to help you decide:

  • Bar Charts: Use for comparing quantities across categories (e.g., sales by product).
  • Line Charts: Best for showing trends over time (e.g., website traffic over months).
  • Pie Charts: Great for showing parts of a whole (e.g., market share distribution).
  • Scatter Plots: Ideal for correlation or relationship between two numeric variables.
  • Radar Charts: Useful for performance reviews across multiple dimensions (e.g., skill ratings).
  • Area Charts: Great for cumulative totals over time (e.g., project cost tracking).
