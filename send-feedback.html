Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I found a mistake/problem on the website.

A: Please fill in the feedback form with details about the problem and the page name to help us fix it.

Q: I saw a bad ad. How can I remove it?

A: Ads are managed by Google and other networks. Click the "x" on the ad to notify the network to remove it. Alternatively, consider installing an ad blocker.

Q: The Chrome vertical scrollbar is missing.

A: Go to chrome://flags/ and disable "Overlay Scrollbars."

Q: I’ve been blocked from accessing the website.

A: This may be due to our bad bot protection. Try accessing from a different device, browser, or IP address. If you use an outdated browser, update it. If using a VPN, disable it or try another IP.

Q: I emailed Easy Converters. When will I get a response?

A: Due to the high volume of emails, we may not be able to respond to all inquiries.

Q: Can I link to Easy Converters from my website?

A: Yes, you are free to link to any page on Easy Converters.

Q: Can I copy content from Easy Converters?

A: No, the content on Easy Converters is copyrighted.

Q: Do you have a smartphone app for the website?

A: No, but most pages are mobile-friendly. You can add a shortcut to your home screen using the "Add to Home Screen" feature.

Q: Do you offer offline access to the website?

A: There is no app for offline use yet, but some pages may be available offline in the future.

Q: I have a question about the content.

A: Feedback is only used for improving the site.

Q: How can I advertise or buy ad space on Easy Converters?

A: You can use Google Ads to display ads on Easy Converters.

Q: Where are the calculations performed?

A: All calculations are done locally on your device.

Q: How do I manage ads on Easy Converters?

A: Easy Converters handles monetization offers and does not require additional ad offers.

Q: Can I print the page or table?

A: Please consider saving paper and avoid printing if possible.

Q: Can I buy Easy Converters?

A: Easy Converters is not for sale. If you'd like to make an offer, please include the amount you wish to offer.
