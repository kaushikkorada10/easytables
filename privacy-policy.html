Privacy Policy

General

We do not sell, rent, or share collected information in ways other than described here.

Google Analytics

Easy Converters uses Google Analytics to monitor site usage and improve page quality. Google Analytics uses cookies and collects statistical, non-personal data.

For more details on how Google uses data, visit: Google Analytics Cookie Usage.

Users can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

Google AdSense

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to display ads based on your previous visits to this site or other sites.

For more information, see: Google Ads Certified External Vendors.

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out.

Snigel

We use a third-party service for site monetization technologies. You can review their privacy and cookie policy here.

To manage your privacy settings, click the button: Manage Consent.

Content Delivery

Easy Converters uses Cloudflare for content delivery. Cloudflare may use cookies to support its functionality. See: Cloudflare Cookies.

Application Data

Easy Converters may store your settings or application data in the browser’s local storage, not on our server. This data will be available for the next session. You can delete the data by clearing the browser’s cache or input data.

User Feedback

User feedback is only used to improve Easy Converters. It is stored in a Gmail account, and will not be shared with third parties. Feedback will be deleted after it’s handled.

Social Share Buttons

Our pages may include social share buttons. Pressing these buttons will share information, like the URL, and other content to social networks.

Multimedia Applications

Some pages may have multimedia features that use the camera and microphone. These will only be activated with your consent. Recorded data may be stored locally, not sent to our server.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.
